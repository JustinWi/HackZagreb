What is #HackZagreb

A hackathon dedicated to building software for the people of Zagreb...by the people of Zagreb.

A civic Hackathon organized by the Founder Institute Zagreb and Development Agency of the City of Zagreb to bring together citizens, entrepreneurs and developers to solve challenges relevant to the citizens of the City of Zagreb. The goal is to collaboratively create and build solutions using publicly released data, code and technology.

Prizes are Awarded to the Top 5 Teams:

  • One year co-working space at the Development Agency of Zagreb
  • 50 hours of consultancy support
  • A seminar from the selection of entrepreneurial seminars given by the Agency
  • Use of Raza's Mobile Lab: a collection of Android, Nokia and iOS devices for testing
  • Use of HP's Demo Lab: stack of servers for testing and demo use.
You'll see we have 5 prizes for 4 teams...Everyone wins! When you join us next time, you'll win too.
