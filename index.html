#HackZagreb
City of Zagreb Hackathon

What is #HackZagreb

A hackathon dedicated to building software for the people of Zagreb...by the people of Zagreb!

A civic Hackathon organized by the Founder Institute Zagreb and Development Agency of the City of Zagreb to bring together citizens, entrepreneurs and developers to solve challenges relevant to the citizens of the City of Zagreb. The goal is to collaboratively create and build solutions using publicly released data, code and technology.

Combine design and coding with food, prizes and a desire to help the people of Zagreb:

  • 10:00: Hackers, designers, artists, environmentalists, etc show up
  • 10:05: Decide what they want to build
  • 10:30: Start building
  • 13:00: Eat lunch (free)
  • 13:10: Keep building
  • 18:00: Eat dinner (free)
  • 18:10: Final touches
  • 20:00: Presentations of finished projects
  • 21:00: Prizes awarded

Prizes are Awarded to the Top 3 Teams:

  • One year co-working space at the Development Agency of Zagreb
  • 50 hours of consultancy support
  • A seminar from the selection of entrepreneurial seminars given by the Agency

Why do People Do This?

  1. They want to build stuff
  2. They want to meet, and work with, smart people
  3. They want to help Zagreb
  4. They want some pretty cool prizes
  5. They like free food
